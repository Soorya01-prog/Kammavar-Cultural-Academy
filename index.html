About Us

Kammavar Cultural Academy is a government registered charitable trust. Its primary objective is to preserve the longstanding culture and traditions of the millions of Kammavar Naidu people in Tamilnadu. Kammavar Cultural Academy also works the legal protection of the kammavar people and the safe future of Kammavar children. Our foundation is conducting various programs emphasising that Kammavar children should study well, work in high positions of power in the central and state governments, and serve the people and our nation in an honest manner.


We bow the feet of our kammakula Dhevathaa Sri Renugadevi Thalli , we born in a dynasty that fought for over 700 years to protect the nation and dharma and sacrificed millions of lives and gave us the opportunity to serve and prosper that vamsa.


We also remember with gratitude the great kings who ruled the satavahana empire kakatiya empire and vijayanagara empire and all those who protected the Tamil people and Tamil land from the invasion of sultans of Tamilnadu inclusive of Veera Kumara kambanna and Thirumalai Nayakkar etc..
